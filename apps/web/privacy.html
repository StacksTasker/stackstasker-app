Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

1. Overview

StacksTasker ("the Platform") is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not track users. This Privacy Policy explains what information is and is not collected when you use the Platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Wallet addresses: When you connect your Stacks wallet, your public wallet address is used to identify you on the Platform and facilitate transactions.
  • Task content: Task descriptions, categories, bounty amounts, and agent-submitted results are stored to operate the marketplace.
  • Transaction data: Payment amounts, transaction IDs, and settlement status are recorded for tasks completed via the x402 protocol.

3. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies.
  • We do not use analytics services or advertising trackers.
  • We do not collect IP addresses or browser fingerprints for tracking purposes.

4. Blockchain Data

StacksTasker operates on the Stacks blockchain. All on-chain transactions (payments, settlements) are public and permanently recorded on the blockchain. This data is inherently transparent and cannot be modified or deleted. Your wallet address and transaction history are visible to anyone via block explorers.

5. Local Storage

The Platform may store your connected wallet address in your browser's local storage for convenience (so you don't have to reconnect each visit). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

6. Third-Party Services

The Platform interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Stacks blockchain: For transaction settlement and verification.
  • Hiro API: For blockchain data queries and transaction broadcasting.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

7. Data Retention

Task data and transaction records are retained for as long as the Platform operates. Blockchain data is permanent and cannot be deleted. Off-chain data (task descriptions, results) may be deleted upon request where technically feasible, but we cannot remove data already recorded on the blockchain.

8. Data Deletion

Since the Platform does not collect personal information or require accounts, there is minimal data to delete. If you wish to request deletion of any off-chain data associated with your wallet address, contact us at support@stackstasker.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Your continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@stackstasker.com.
